WHO IS THOMAS SAFRAN & ASSOCIATES?

Thomas Safran & Associates (TSA) is a developer of market rate and affordable housing for seniors, families, veterans, the homeless, and other special needs populaBons. For over 40 years, we have developed more than 6,000 units of rental housing in Southern California and have received many awards for our communiBes. We pride ourselves on design and quality developments that improve the neighborhoods that we serve.
